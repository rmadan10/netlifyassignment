BUDT748 INDUSTRY PRACTICUM
One of my courses this semester is Industry Practicum (BUDT748), where we collaborate with real organizations to address their business challenges. Rather than traditional lectures, the class functions like a consulting engagement—my team gathers requirements, designs solutions, and develops prototypes that clients can put into practice. It’s a hands-on opportunity to apply everything I’ve learned in the MSIS program and create impact beyond the classroom.
Professor: Paul T. Shapiro
